üìã About the Campaign

What is this advertising service?
We run a monthly direct mail campaign delivering professionally printed ads to 5,000 homes in Southwest Calgary's Fish Creek area. Your business is featured alongside a select group of non-competing local service providers, giving you premium exposure at a fraction of traditional direct mail costs.
When do the mailers go out?
Mailers are delivered on the 15th of each month.
How many businesses are featured?
Each mailer includes 8-11 businesses (or up to 22 for single-sided ads), with strict category exclusivity.

üéØ Category Exclusivity

What does "category exclusivity" mean?
Only ONE business per category is featured on each mailer. If you're a plumber, you're the only plumber. No competing businesses will appear alongside yours.
What if my category is already taken?
Categories are first-come, first-served. You can:
  • Reserve your spot for the following month
  • Choose a related category if applicable
  • Sign up for multi-month to guarantee your spot
